Loan Types

Lender categories the agent matches to

🏦

DSCR Rental Loans

For buy-and-hold investors. Qualification based on property income, not personal income. Best fit when DSCR ≥ 1.0 and LTV ≤ 80%.

🔨

Hard Money / Fix-and-Flip

Short-term asset-backed financing for rehab deals. Based on ARV and rehab experience. Fast close, higher rates, lower LTV standards.

🌉

Bridge Financing

Short-term loans for transitional assets — value-add, lease-up, or stabilization. Typical terms 12–36 months.

🏢

Conventional Investment

Fannie/Freddie investment property products. Best for stabilized 1–4 unit rentals with strong borrower profile.

🏙️

Multifamily Agency

Fannie Mae / Freddie Mac / HUD products for 5+ unit properties. Lower rates, longer amortization, more paperwork.

📁

Portfolio Lending

Community bank and credit union products held in-house. Flexible criteria, relationship-based, often best for unusual deals that don't fit standard boxes.

FAQ

Common questions

Why does lender matching matter? ▾
Different lenders specialize in different deal types. Applying to the wrong lender wastes time, generates hard credit pulls, and reduces your chance of approval. A DSCR lender won't fund a fix-and-flip. A conventional lender won't do a 0.75 DSCR deal. Matching to the right lender type first dramatically improves approval odds and speeds up closing.
Does Underlytix refer me to specific lenders? ▾
Underlytix identifies the right loan type and lender category for your deal. The Lender Portal allows lenders to receive matched deal submissions directly. The system is designed to connect qualified deals with appropriate lenders — reducing friction on both sides.
