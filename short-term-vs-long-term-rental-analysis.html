WHY IT MATTERS

The strategy changes the deal, not just the income

A property can cash-flow beautifully as a short-term rental and barely break even as a long-term hold — or the reverse. But higher STR income comes with higher expenses, more volatility, regulatory exposure, and different lender treatment. Choosing the wrong strategy doesn’t just lower returns; it can make the deal unfinanceable. The comparison has to happen before the offer, not after closing.

COMMON QUESTIONS

STR vs LTR analysis questions

Short-term vs long-term rental — which is better? ▾
It depends on the specific property. Short-term rentals usually generate higher gross income but carry higher expenses, more volatility, and regulatory risk; long-term rentals are steadier and simpler to finance. Underlytix models both on the same property so the choice is based on numbers, not assumptions.
Does a short-term rental finance differently than a long-term rental? ▾
Yes. Lenders often treat short-term rental income differently, applying haircuts or extra documentation, and some DSCR programs exclude it. The same property can clear DSCR as a long-term rental and fail as a short-term one, which is why Underlytix tests financeability under both strategies.
How do I compare STR and LTR cash flow? ▾
Model each income stream with its own realistic expenses: short-term needs cleaning, furnishing, management, utilities, and a seasonal vacancy assumption, while long-term uses market rent and standard costs. Underlytix produces both side by side, including the financing outcome, in about 60 seconds.
What regulatory risks affect short-term rentals? ▾
Local ordinances, HOA rules, and permit limits can restrict or ban rentals under 30 days, which can invalidate an STR model entirely. Underlytix flags when regulatory exposure materially changes the recommendation so it is not discovered after purchase.
Can Underlytix tell me to pass on a property? ▾
Yes. If neither rental strategy produces a financeable, adequately performing deal, the recommendation is to pass. Underlytix is designed to filter out weak deals early, not to justify every purchase.
