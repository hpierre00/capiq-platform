FAQ

Common questions

What deal types does the agent support? ▾
Buy-and-hold rental properties (SFR, multifamily, mixed-use), fix-and-flip acquisitions, and BRRRR strategy deals. Each type uses appropriate scoring criteria — rental properties use DSCR; fix-and-flip deals use ARV and rehab analysis.
Does the agent pull my credit? ▾
No. The Deal Analysis Agent scores the deal based on property metrics and financial inputs only. No credit pull, no SSN, no personal financial documents required.
How accurate is the Capital Readiness Score? ▾
The score is based on the inputs you provide. Accuracy depends on the accuracy of your rent estimates, expense projections, and loan parameters. The score is designed to match the criteria used by DSCR lenders, hard money lenders, and conventional investment property lenders.
