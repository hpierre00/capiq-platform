COMMON QUESTIONS

Realtor buyer prequalification questions

What is buyer prequalification for realtors? ▾
It is a fast, upfront check of whether a buyer can actually finance a purchase at a given price — based on income, debts, down payment, and program fit — done before you invest time in showings or an offer. Underlytix produces this readiness read in about 60 seconds without a credit pull or a loan application.
Can a realtor show property without a preapproval letter? ▾
Yes. There is no legal requirement for a preapproval letter to show a home, though many agents prefer some proof of financing before investing time. A readiness read from Underlytix gives you that signal instantly when a lender preapproval is not yet in hand or a loan officer is unavailable.
How does Underlytix evaluate buyer readiness? ▾
It models the full PITIA payment, debt-to-income ratio, and reserves against real financing thresholds for conventional, FHA, VA, and DSCR programs, then returns whether the buyer is fundable at the target price, at a lower price, or not yet — naming the specific constraint.
Is Underlytix a replacement for a lender preapproval? ▾
No. Underlytix is pre-application intelligence, not a commitment to lend. A lender preapproval still requires a credit pull and underwriting. Underlytix tells you whether pursuing that preapproval is worth the buyer’s and your time.
Can Underlytix help when a mortgage broker is unavailable? ▾
Yes. That is one of its most common uses. When it is after hours, a weekend, or your loan officer is not answering, you can still get an objective read on buyer strength in about a minute and decide whether to move forward on an offer.
