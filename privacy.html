Privacy Policy

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We use your data only to operate the platform, improve our services, and communicate with you about your account.

1. Who We Are

Underlytix LLC ("Underlytix," "we," "us," or "our") operates the capital intelligence platform at underlytix.com (the "Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the Platform.

For privacy questions, contact us at support@underlytix.com.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing the Platform: Running AI deal analysis, generating prequalification estimates, matching investors to lenders, and displaying results.
  • Account management: Authentication, password reset, usage tracking against plan limits.
  • Communications: Welcome emails, analysis completion notifications, billing receipts, and account alerts. We use Resend to deliver transactional emails.
  • Platform improvement: Understanding how users interact with features to improve reliability and accuracy.
  • Fraud prevention and compliance: Detecting misuse, enforcing our Terms of Service, and complying with applicable law.
  • Operator reporting: Aggregated, anonymized metrics used internally to measure platform health (deal volume, pass rates, geographic distribution).

4. Client Data Entered by Realtors

When realtors use the Realtor Portal, they enter information about their clients (buyers). This data is processed to generate prequalification estimates.

  • Client data is stored in our database associated with the realtor's account, not with any individual consumer record.
  • We do not use client data to contact, profile, or market to any individual consumer.
  • Realtors are responsible for obtaining any consents required by their jurisdiction before entering client information into the Platform.
  • Client data is retained for 24 months from the date of entry or until the realtor's account is deleted, whichever comes first.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Stripe, Inc.: Payment processing. Stripe receives your email and subscription details. Stripe's Privacy Policy governs their use of this data.
  • Supabase, Inc.: Database and backend infrastructure. Your data is stored in Supabase-managed PostgreSQL databases on AWS infrastructure.
  • Anthropic, PBC: AI analysis engine. Deal details and prequal chat messages are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate analysis. Anthropic does not use API-submitted data to train its models per their API Terms.
  • Resend, Inc.: Transactional email delivery. Your email address is shared with Resend to deliver account and notification emails.
  • Notion Labs, Inc.: CRM and operator pipeline. Deal submissions and lender activity are synced to an internal Notion workspace accessible only to Underlytix operators.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Underlytix, its users, or the public.
  • Business transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

7. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Passwords are hashed before storage and never stored in plaintext.
  • Authentication tokens are signed and time-limited.
  • Database access is restricted to authorized application services.
  • Netlify edge functions and Supabase edge functions handle sensitive operations server-side, not in the browser.

No system is completely secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at support@underlytix.com.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data. Billing records may be retained for legal compliance periods.
  • Portability: Request export of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out of non-essential emails: You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time. Transactional emails (receipts, alerts) cannot be disabled while your account is active.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@underlytix.com from the email address associated with your account. We will respond within 30 days.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, share, and sell (we do not sell).
  • The right to delete personal information we have collected.
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal information.
  • The right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell or share for advertising).
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

To submit a CCPA request, email support@underlytix.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.
