FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a lender to use Underlytix? ▾
No. Underlytix is fully independent of any lender. You run deal analysis, get your score, and review lender match recommendations — all without contacting a lender. You only engage a lender when you have a deal that scores fundable and you're ready to move forward.
Is Underlytix only for experienced investors? ▾
No. Underlytix is designed for investors at every stage — from first deal to portfolio scale. First-time investors benefit from understanding DSCR and fundability before wasting time on their first bad deal. Experienced investors benefit from speed and consistency when screening high volumes of deals.
What does it cost? ▾
Underlytix offers a free tier to get started. Paid plans unlock unlimited deal analyses, full risk reporting, and lender match access. See full pricing at underlytix.com/#pricing.
How is this different from just using a spreadsheet? ▾
A spreadsheet requires you to know the right formulas, use the right benchmarks, and interpret results against real lender criteria. Underlytix pre-loads lender benchmarks, calculates every metric automatically, and scores the deal against real financing products. The result is a fundability verdict — not just numbers that you still have to interpret.
